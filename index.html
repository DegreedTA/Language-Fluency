Before you record

This is a chance for us to hear how you communicate in English in a real professional context — not how well you can prepare for an assessment.

The most helpful recordings are the ones where someone just speaks naturally, the way they would in an actual conversation. The more rehearsed or scripted it sounds, the less useful it is for us.

We're listening for fluency and confidence, not perfect grammar or a specific accent. Think of it as a low-stakes conversation, not a test.
